About Her

Lilly is a charismatic and energised dog. She is a black labrador and is known to be quite a handful, but it pays off as she has an affectionate and loving nature to her.

What she likes

Lilly loves walks and running around at the dog park with her friends. She loves to play fetch and is always looking for a treat.

What she dislikes

Lilly hates waiting for things, she can't wait and always goes haywire when she can't have something that she wants. She also dislikes people touching her face and playing with it as it is very annoying. She also dislikes the rain as it means that she can't go outside and play.
